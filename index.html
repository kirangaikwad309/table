About Us

DCC is platform to share and discuss scientific information and practical aspects in the management of Diabetes, cardiology and related complications. In day-today clinical practice we strongly felt that there is need to create platform to bridge the gap between research and clinical practice.

What is Our Goal?

To bridge the gap between research and clinical practice.

Event Details

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you to the 1st Diabetes- Controversies to Consensus Conference (DCC 2018), initiative of DNA (Diabetes Nutrition Awareness) Academy which is being organized at Queen of all Hill Stations – Mahabaleshwar, at the exotic Hotel Dreamland from 10th Feb to 11th Feb 2018. We strongly believe that any Meeting would be academic feast in true sense when it provides more clinical practice oriented content and helps us in the most crucial part of clinical practice which is decision making, when we go back to our consulting chambers. Medicine is the evolving science and science of controversies, possibilities and probabilities. Aim of the meeting is to create platform which delivers most practical content to bridge the gap between research and clinical practice, in the fields of Diabetology. Meeting involves controversies related diabetes management , global clinical trials data relevant to clinical practice, new molecules development and their respective place in clinical practice, consensus, cutting edge technologies in diagnostic and management , workshops, new hypothesis, preventive strategies and current guidelines. Key Opinion Leaders (KOL) in this field will be sharing their knowledge and experience which we are sure will be of great help in decision making in our consulting chambers.
